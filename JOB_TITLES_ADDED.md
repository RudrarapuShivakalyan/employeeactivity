## Benefits of Specific Job Titles

✅ **Clear Role Definition** - Each employee has a specific, professional job title
✅ **Career Progression** - Titles reflect experience levels (Junior, Senior, Lead, Manager)
✅ **Department Alignment** - Job titles match department functions
✅ **Search Enhancement** - Users can search by specific roles like "DevOps Engineer"
✅ **Professional Standards** - Industry-standard job titles used throughout
✅ **Organizational Clarity** - Clear hierarchy and specialization visible

## Implementation Details

- **Field Added:** `jobTitle` to all employee, manager, and admin records
- **Display Location:** Personal Information section, third field after Name and Employee ID
- **Fallback Value:** "Not specified" if job title is missing
- **Search Integration:** Job titles are searchable through the name search field
- **Data Consistency:** All 30 members now have specific, professional job titles
